Mission Statement

We are a collection of professionals volunteering time towards the creation of a transparent, crowdsourced platform for analyzing and qualifying public information.

Non-Partisan

Impartiality and neutrality are two key ingredients for creating an environment where information can remain free from degredation and distortion. AltVeracity isn't a spy network and we aren't the news, we only seek to qualify information and sources without interference from third parties.

The Verax Score

Verax means to 'speak truthfully' and altVeracity is developing an algorithm for transparently scoring information, sources, contributors, and reviewers.

Trust is Basic

You do not have to trust what you can verify yourself, but without dilligence and scholastic discipline, separating fact from fiction can be a daunting process.

Data Aggregation

From data we gather information, from information comes awareness, from awareness we derive facts, but knowledge is always the goal.

Accuracy Consensus

The most pivotal element of altVeracity is the community, the verified contributors, researchers and reviewers who vote on matters of accuracy, validation and approval. The only content published by altVeracity is what the community deems Release Worthy.

Visualization

Building a transparent data collection operation, one that allows independent contribution under established procedures is trivial without the ability to drive meaningful analysis. Data Reports generated by the altVeracity platform can be used in newsworthy publications or by private groups and even citizens.
